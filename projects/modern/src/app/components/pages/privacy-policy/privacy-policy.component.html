Privacy Policy

  • The platform uses Stripe to process and manage all finance and payment related activities; the platform (Carry) does not keep or maintain any personal finance information such as bank accounts, credit/debit cards, etc.
  • The platform does/will not sell any user data; all information within the app exists only within the app solefy for functionality. Any changes will be communicated to users of the platform prior.
  • Users will only see information pertaining to their service/customer related functions within the app.
  • Users will/may not share or disclose personal information about deliveries they provide. This includes but is not limited to: addresses, names, etc.
  • This app is intended for people age 18 or older.
  • Stripe collects identity information to verify service providers; that information is NOT shared with the platform; Stripe will merely indicate if a service provider's verification/identity is valid or not.
